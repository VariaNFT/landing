Quickstart

Prepare a template and your data

Export your design as an template SVG file create a CSV file as data source. You can design template with most software (with Adobe Illustrator being the most ideal) and process your data with Office Excel or Google Spreadsheet.

Setup your project

Configure the metadata of your NFTs and pick Rarible or your own contract to mint. VariaNFT uses contracts from Rarible Protocol, which are strongly supported by experienced engineers.

Mint your NFTs to people

Upload and send NFTs out to others with one simple click. Files will be stored on IPFS and supported by NFT.storage, made by Protocol Labs.
